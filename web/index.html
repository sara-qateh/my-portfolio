About Me

I am a passionate web developer with an interest in building clean and user-friendly websites. I enjoy learning new technologies and improving my skills. I have a diverse academic background shaped by resilience and a strong commitment to learning. I initially studied Law and Political Science, reaching my final year; however, my education was interrupted when girls were banned from attending universities in Afghanistan. Despite these challenges, I remained committed to my academic growth and continued developing my skills independently. Later, I pursued Computer Science at AUAF majoring software engineering. Now, I am a senior sudent at AUAF. I have been taught English through online platforms for Afghan girls since 2021.
